-   Power of two
    
    -   An ASCII character is one byte (8 bits)
    -   2 ^ 10 ~ 1 K = 1 KB 
    -   2 ^ 20 ~ 1 M = 1 MB
    -   2 ^ 30 ~ 1 B = 1 GB
    -   2 ^ 40 ~ 1 T = 1 TB
    -   2 ^ 50 ~ 1 Q = 1 PB
